How to Add Your Videos:

  1. Go to your YouTube video
  2. Click "Share" below the video
  3. Click "Embed" (optional) or just copy the video ID from the URL
  4. The video ID is the part after "v=" (e.g., youtube.com/watch?v=dQw4w9WgXcQ)
  5. Replace YOUR_VIDEO_ID in the iframe src with your video ID
